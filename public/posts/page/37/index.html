.NET DataSet dump (C#)
One thing that I really like about ColdFusion is the powerful cfdump. Use the cfdump tag to get the elements, variables, and values of most kinds of ColdFusion objects. Useful for debugging. You can display the contents of simple and complex variables, objects, components, user-defined functions, and other elements.  Of all the tags in ColdFusion, cfoutput is probably the only tag that I use more.
