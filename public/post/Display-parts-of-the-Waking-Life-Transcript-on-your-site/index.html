Display parts of the Waking Life Transcript on your site

  • September 1, 2008
  • James Skemp
  • dvd / movie, philosophy

A lot of people like to pull full passages from Waking Life, as it's a very quotable movie.

However, the problem with that is that if things change there's a whole lot of sites that are out-of-date. And, sometimes people forget to add attribution ...

I've been thinking about ways to make this easier, and believe I've come up with an idea.

Looking at the numbers, I'm working from the most popular to the least popular chapters, with the goal of having all available. The full chapters are available, at least. I'll be adding chapter parts as time permits / demand warrants.
