New gallery posted

After a bit of looking around, for the best gallery application, I've settled on SlideShowPro. While it does require Flash (version 7 or higher, but I'm requiring version 8 or higher for mine), and it did cost some money, it's a great little application.

You can see what I've currently got available at http://strivinglife.net/gallery/.

There's still a lot I've got to play with, but, it's at least got a start of 12 images (at this time). The gallery requires a pretty fast connection, but it is what it is.
