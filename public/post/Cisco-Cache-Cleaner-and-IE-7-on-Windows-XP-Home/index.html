Cisco Cache Cleaner and IE 7 on Windows XP Home

  • February 17, 2008
  • James Skemp
  • Internet

If you have the pleasure of running Cisco's Cache Cleaner on your home computer, you may run into an issue with your settings being set to something other than you'd prefer.

While it may appear that Cache Cleaner clears out your data, it in fact only appears to turn auto-complete off.

In Internet Explorer 7, go to Tools > Internet Options > Content > AutoComplete > Settings and turn the settings you want back on.

Gurr.
