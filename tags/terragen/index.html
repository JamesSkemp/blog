Terragen&trade; 2 Technology Preview to be released December 15

  • December 15, 2006
  • James Skemp
Development on Planetside Software's Terragen&trade; has been extremely slow in the last couple of years.  While new versions and news were coming out at a semi-steady pace years ago, visible development dried up. All that changes on December 15 (possibly) with the release of the Terragen 2 Technology Preview.  It's almost 7:30 CST, which means December 16 in a number of parts of the world, and there's no sign of the download yet.  The fact that there's no mention of what time, makes me think that the Preview still needs/needed a few more touches before it was released.  Or, they just want to do some load testing on their servers.  Your call.
